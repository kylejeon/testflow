## 2. Free Trial

- During the free trial period, **no charges are made**.
- You may cancel at any time during the trial at no cost.

## 3. 14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you are not satisfied with your first paid subscription (Starter, Professional, or Enterprise), you may request a full refund within **14 calendar days** of the initial charge, provided:

- This is your **first paid subscription** for this account
- The request is submitted within 14 days of the charge date
- You have not previously received a refund for Testably

**How to request:** Email {{support_email}} with subject line "Refund Request" and include your account email and order ID (found in your Paddle receipt).

We will process eligible refunds within **5–10 business days** to your original payment method.

## 4. Monthly Subscriptions

For monthly subscriptions after the 14-day money-back period:

- **Cancellation:** You may cancel at any time. Access continues until the end of the current billing period. **No partial refunds** are issued for unused days in the current month.
- **Downgrade:** Downgrading to a lower tier takes effect at the next billing cycle. No refund for the difference in the current period.

---

## 5. Annual Subscriptions

For annual subscriptions after the 14-day money-back period:

- **Within 30 days of payment:** Eligible for a **pro-rated refund** for the unused months remaining (calculated from the cancellation date to the end of the annual term, rounded down to full months).
- **After 30 days of payment:** No refunds are issued. Access continues until the end of the annual term.
- **Downgrade:** Contact {{support_email}} to discuss options. Refunds for annual downgrades are evaluated case by case.

### Pro-rated Refund Calculation Example
> Annual plan paid January 1. Cancellation requested February 20 (after 14-day no-questions-asked window but within 30 days).  
> Months used: 2 (January + February). Months remaining: 10.  
> Refund = (Annual price / 12) × 10 remaining months.

## 6. Non-Refundable Cases

The following are not eligible for refunds:

- Charges older than 30 days (unless covered by the 14-day guarantee)
- Accounts suspended or terminated for Terms of Service violations
- Requests where the account has experienced significant usage inconsistent with a legitimate refund claim
- Promotional or discounted pricing (e.g., launch pricing, coupon codes), unless mandated by law
- Add-on charges or one-time purchases, if any

EU consumers have a statutory 14-day right of withdrawal for digital services, which is waived upon first use of the Service. By starting to use the Service, you expressly consent to performance beginning and acknowledge that you lose your withdrawal right upon use.
